Global Response Timeline

February 21, 2022

Russian President Vladimir Putin appears on Russian state television to deliver a speech in which he recognizes the “independence and sovereignty” of the Donetsk People’s Republic and the Lugansk People’s Republic, both of which are located in the Donbas region of southeastern Ukraine.

United States President Joseph R. Biden, Jr., signs Executive Order 14065, titled "Blocking Property of Certain Persons and Prohibiting Certain Transactions With Respect to Continued Russian Efforts To Undermine the Sovereignty and Territorial Integrity of Ukraine." 87 Fed. Reg. 10293 (Feb. 23, 2022).

February 24, 2022

The United States Department of the Treasury’s Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) sanctions various Belarusian state entities and officials over their support of Russia’s invasion of Ukraine. The sanctioned Belarusian entities and individuals are as follows:

  • Belarussian Bank of Development and Reconstruction Belinvestbank Joint Stock Company (Belinvestbank)
    • Headquarters: Minsk, Belarus.
    • Industry: Finance
    • Reason for Sanction: Fourth largest financial institution in Belarus; being owned or controlled by, or for having acted or purported to act for or on behalf of, directly or indirectly, the Government of Belarus.
    • Authority: Executive Order 14038.
  • Limited Liability Company Belinvest-Engineering
    • Headquarters: Minsk, Belarus.
    • Industry: Real Estate Firm
    • Reason for Sanction: Being owned or controlled by, or for having acted or purported to act for or on behalf of, directly or indirectly, Belinvestbank.
    • Authority: Executive Order 14038.
  • CJSC Belbizneslizing
    • Headquarters: Minsk, Belarus.
    • Industry: Financial Leasing Company
    • Reason for Sanction: Being owned or controlled by, or for having acted or purported to act for or on behalf of, directly or indirectly, Belinvestbank.
    • Authority: Executive Order 14038.

March 2, 2022

The United States Federal Aviation Administration issued Special Security Instructions (SSI) prohibiting within the territorial airpsace of the United States all air operations with any connection to Russia or Russian nationals. The immediate effect of the SSI was the grounding and prohibition of all commercial Russian airlines and the personal jets of Russian oligarchs from the skies of the United States.
